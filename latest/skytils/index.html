    Skytils Alternatives¶

    Skytils is a Hypixel Skyblock mod available on both 1.8.9 and 1.21. In the 1.21 version, it continues its practice of bundling Essential, which gives the user no ability to remove it, despite both the issues with Essential and the fact that it is fully unnecessary.

    Additionally, Skytils is not not receiving updates, and the releases for older versions are reportedly extremely buggy and unstable.

    For more information on exactly why Essential is bad and it is considered such a party foul to bundle it, check out the Essential Alternatives Page.
